Who We Are

A forge born in darkness.

IronPact Studios is an independent game development and digital asset studio from Mexico, built on the belief that the most compelling worlds are the ones that feel dangerous, ancient, and alive.

We create 2D pixel art environments, characters, and full game experiences rooted in dark fantasy — templars, demons, forgotten kingdoms, and the grim pacts made between them. Our work spans hand-crafted asset packs for the game development community to fully realized games that push the genre forward.

As we grow, so does our vision — from 2D pixel art to 3D environments and beyond. Every release is a step deeper into the dark.

✦
